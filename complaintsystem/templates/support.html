What To Include

Good complaint details reduce back-and-forth and help staff resolve issues faster.

Clear title

Use a specific title like Hostel Block A water leak.

Exact location

Add room, lab, classroom, bus route, or library section.

Useful evidence

Upload an image or file when it helps explain the problem.

Correct priority

Use High for urgent safety, access, or major disruption issues.

Status Flow

Every complaint follows a simple visible path.

01Pending

Complaint is submitted and waiting for staff assignment.

02Assigned

The system or admin connects it to the right staff team.

03In Progress

Staff are working on the issue and can add remarks.

04Resolved

The complaint is completed and stored in history.
